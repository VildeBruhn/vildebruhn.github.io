# Exercise 1

---

In this exercise, we will use parts of a dataset from [Jensen _et. al_ (2022)](https://www.pnas.org/doi/abs/10.1073/pnas.2207634119), looking into body size adaptations of a wild population of Atlantic salmon (_Salmo salar_) in a river impacted by hydropower development. The time series used here can also be downloaded (in raw version) from the [PETS database]({% link /other_resources/PETS.md %}).
